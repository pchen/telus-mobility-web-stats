A look into some of the screens visiting TELUS in July 2013

As a companion to the post I wrote for the TELUS Digital Labs blog on designing responsively, I thought that I would dig into real stats from TELUS' web analytics and find out who (or rather, what) has been looking at our site. Have fun exploring the interactive * infographic above.

Some interesting stats highlighted below:

Mobile versus Desktop

  • 20% of page views on telusmobility.com were from mobile devices.
    • This is up from 14% in 2012, and 8% in 2011.
    • Assuming a steady increase of 6% each year, could this mean that mobile devices will account for half of the traffic by 2018 or even sooner?

Screen resolution long tail

  • 1,532 unique device models accounted for 8 million page views.
    • Only 99 device models generated 97.5% of the views.
    • A very loooong tail of 1433 devices account for the rest.
